WHERE IS CHRISTMAS?! ESSENTIAL QUESTION Much of our world is lost in the commercialism of Christmas. Your challenge is to develop a solution to bring back the true meaning of Christmas. Task 2 Christmas Now and Then Your task is to investigate the experience of Christmas and whether or not it has changed over the years. You must then present your findings in an app of your choice. You will need to develop a set of research questions to gather your information. Suggestions for gathering your information are: • Develop an electronic survey • Interview family member by videoing them. Sample research questions; • How do you celebrate Christmas? • Has the way you celebrate Christmas changed over time and if so, how? • What is your biggest focus at Christmas time? • What do you most look forward to at Christmas time? • What do you find challenging at Christmas time? • If you could change one thing at Christmas what would it be?
